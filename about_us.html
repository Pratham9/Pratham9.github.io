A little about us!


BubbleTime tea is Kamloops very own homegrown tea shop. It all began with the business partnership of two local tea connoisseurs who recognized the need for an establishment specializing in the art of tea. BubbleTime is led by 15 years of food management and promises to deliver only top quality and the freshest of leaf-based teas made to each order.

Our everyday mission is to ensure that each and every tea lover is greeted with a smile and treated with a passion.
